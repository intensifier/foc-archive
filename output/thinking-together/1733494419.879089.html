# Denny Vrandečić 2024-12-13 00:07
For a large domain I am not a friend of human-readable unique identifiers, but prefer meaningless unique identifiers coupled with a system to label or name things in different languages.
# Nilesh Trivedi 2024-12-13 04:22
@Denny Vrandečić Yeah, I often reconsider this choice. In fact, for items like books or courses, I used (uuid + labels) without requiring these labels to be identifiers.
But for topics/subjects, I think identifers are a net benefit. Languages are effective because people have shared meanings for words. By adding uniqueness and removing ambiguity, we get to leverage this aspect. It is very annoying to have people who use the topic tag "math" v/s "maths" v/s "mathematics" can't find each other's data - unless we invest time/effort in building and maintaining a high-quality search engine.
